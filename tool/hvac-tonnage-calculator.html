How to use

Enter a load that has already been calculated. This tool converts the unit; it does not determine building load or select equipment.

Formula

Refrigeration tons = BTU/h ÷ 12,000

Variables

One refrigeration ton is 12,000 BTU/h of cooling capacity.

Example

24,000 BTU/h is 2 refrigeration tons.

Limits and disclaimer

Equipment performance varies with conditions; do not equate the unit conversion with final equipment selection.

FAQ

Is tonnage electrical input?

No. It is a thermal capacity convention.
