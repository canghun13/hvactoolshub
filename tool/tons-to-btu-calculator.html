Formula

BTU/h = refrigeration tons × 12,000

Variables

A refrigeration ton is the conventional equivalent of 12,000 BTU/h of capacity.

Example

1.5 refrigeration tons × 12,000 = 18,000 BTU/h.

FAQ

Can I use this for equipment electrical demand?

No. Electrical demand requires the equipment input rating and efficiency data.
