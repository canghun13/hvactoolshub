Convert BTU/h to tons

One refrigeration ton is 12,000 BTU/h.

Use
Capacity comparison

Formula

Refrigeration tons = BTU/h ÷ 12,000

Variables

BTU/h is the rated heat-transfer capacity. Refrigeration tons are a conventional HVAC capacity unit.

Example

24,000 BTU/h ÷ 12,000 = 2 refrigeration tons.

FAQ

Is tonnage electrical power?

No. It is cooling capacity, not electrical input power.
