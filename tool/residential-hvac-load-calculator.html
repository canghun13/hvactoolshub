How to use

Use only with a documented scenario. ACCA Manual J is the recognized residential load-calculation procedure and considers far more inputs.

Calculation method

Planning load = area × selected rate × adjustment

Variables

Climate, insulation, air leakage, windows, orientation, occupancy, and ventilation all affect residence loads.

Example

1,800 ft² × 20 × 1.00 = 36,000 BTU/h.

Limits and disclaimer

Not a Manual J calculation; do not use alone for equipment selection.

FAQ

Does square footage determine residential size?

No. It is only one input among many.
