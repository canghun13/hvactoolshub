Rectangular Duct Size Calculator

Calculate a preliminary rectangular duct width and height.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Calculator

Calculated sizein (W × H)

How to use

Set airflow, target velocity, and a practical width-to-height ratio. The calculator derives dimensions with the same face area.

Formula

Area = 144 × CFM ÷ FPM; H = √(Area ÷ ratio); W = ratio × H

Variables

Area is in square inches; ratio is width divided by height.

Example

800 CFM at 700 FPM with a 2:1 ratio produces approximately 21.6 × 10.8 in.

Result interpretation

Verify the selected duct's equivalent diameter and pressure loss; a very high aspect ratio can increase friction and fabrication constraints.

FAQ

Can I use any aspect ratio?

Physical space and design criteria govern the choice. Check the final selection against your duct design method.
