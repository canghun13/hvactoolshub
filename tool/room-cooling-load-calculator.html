Calculator

Preliminary room loadBTU/h

How to use

Record why the rate and adjustment differ for window exposure, glazing, shade, ceiling height, people, equipment, infiltration, or ventilation.

Calculation method

Room load = area × selected rate × room adjustment

Variables

Area alone cannot represent solar, internal, latent, or airflow loads.

Example

180 ft² × 20 × 1.00 = 3,600 BTU/h.

Limits and disclaimer

This does not allocate a complete building load or replace detailed room-by-room analysis.

FAQ

Can one room rate apply to every room?

No. Exposure and use vary by room.
