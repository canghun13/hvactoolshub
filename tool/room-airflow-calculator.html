Room Airflow Calculator

Calculate a transparent room airflow planning value.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Room airflowCFM

How to use

Enter finished interior dimensions and a project ACH.

Formula

CFM = length × width × height × ACH ÷ 60

Variables

Dimensions form room volume; ACH reflects the intended air-exchange rate.

Example

12 × 10 × 8 ft at 6 ACH equals 96 CFM.

Result interpretation

Use this for preliminary planning; final HVAC design depends on the ventilation purpose and applicable code.

FAQ

Does room airflow equal cooling airflow?

Not necessarily. Cooling, outdoor-air, exhaust, and filtration airflow are distinct design questions.
