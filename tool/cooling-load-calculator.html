How to use

Use a rate from a documented scenario. Adjust only when you can describe the solar, orientation, glazing, occupancy, equipment, or ventilation condition behind it.

Calculation method

Cooling estimate = area × chosen rate × adjustment

Variables

Cooling loads include external and internal heat gains, not just floor area.

Example

500 ft² × 20 × 1.00 = 10,000 BTU/h.

Limits and disclaimer

This does not calculate latent load, hourly solar gain, or full cooling equipment selection.

FAQ

Does it replace a cooling load calculation?

No. Use an appropriate Manual J or ASHRAE procedure for final design.
