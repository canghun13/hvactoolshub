Formula

BTU/h per ft² = rated BTU/h ÷ floor area (ft²)

Variables

Use the nameplate or published capacity and the actual conditioned floor area.

Example

12,000 BTU/h over 500 ft² equals 24 BTU/h per ft².

FAQ

Does this tell me what size system to buy?

No. It only reports a ratio. Actual loads depend on construction, climate, solar gain, occupants, and ventilation.
