Duct Cross-Sectional Area Calculator

Calculate the face area of a round duct from its inside diameter.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Calculator

Cross-sectional areaft²

How to use

Enter the duct's inside diameter. Use face area with CFM to check velocity.

Formula

Area = π × (D ÷ 12)² ÷ 4

Variables

D is diameter in inches; area is reported in square feet and square inches.

Example

A 12 in round duct has an area of about 0.785 ft² or 113.1 in².

Result interpretation

Interior dimensions, liners, and fittings affect the effective flow path.

FAQ

Can I use outside duct dimensions?

Use inside dimensions for airflow area whenever available.
