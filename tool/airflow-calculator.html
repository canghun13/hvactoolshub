Airflow Calculator

Calculate CFM from room dimensions and an ACH target.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

AirflowCFM

How to use

Enter internal room dimensions and the project ACH.

Formula

CFM = room volume × ACH ÷ 60

Variables

Room volume is ft³; ACH is changes per hour.

Example

A 960 ft³ room at 6 ACH needs 96 CFM.

Result interpretation

Confirm actual airflow, ventilation type, codes, and equipment performance.

FAQ

Is this a final design value?

No. It is a preliminary airflow calculation.
