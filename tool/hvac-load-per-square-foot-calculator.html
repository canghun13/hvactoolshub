HVAC Load per Square Foot Calculator

Calculate a load-to-area ratio from a known load result.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Calculator

Load ratioBTU/h per ft²

How to use

Enter a previously determined load and its conditioned area. Use the result to compare documented scenarios, not as a universal sizing prescription.

Formula

Load per ft² = total load (BTU/h) ÷ conditioned area (ft²)

Variables

The ratio inherits all the assumptions and limitations of the original load calculation.

Example

24,000 BTU/h ÷ 1,200 ft² = 20 BTU/h per ft².

Limits and disclaimer

Identical ratios can conceal very different climates, envelope conditions, loads, and ventilation requirements.

FAQ

Can this ratio size another building?

Not by itself. Use a separate, project-specific load calculation.
