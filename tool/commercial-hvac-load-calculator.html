How to use

Commercial loads need documented occupancy, lights, equipment, schedules, ventilation, envelope, and solar assumptions. Treat this as a transparent starting estimate only.

Calculation method

Planning load = area × selected rate × adjustment

Variables

Internal gains and outdoor air can be major commercial load drivers.

Example

5,000 ft² × 25 × 1.00 = 125,000 BTU/h.

Limits and disclaimer

Not an ASHRAE heat-balance or RTS calculation.

FAQ

Can office and retail use the same rate?

Not reliably; schedules, people, lighting, equipment, and ventilation can differ.
