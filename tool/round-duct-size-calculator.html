Round Duct Size Calculator

Calculate a preliminary round duct diameter for a required airflow.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Calculator

Round duct sizein diameter

How to use

Use a known CFM and design velocity to calculate the area and equivalent round diameter.

Formula

D = 12 × √(4 × (CFM ÷ FPM) ÷ π)

Variables

Airflow is CFM; velocity is FPM; diameter is inches.

Example

400 CFM at 600 FPM calculates to about a 11.1 in round diameter.

Result interpretation

Use the next appropriate available duct size, then verify the resulting velocity and pressure loss.

FAQ

Does this include elbows or fittings?

No. It is a straight-duct area calculation.
