How to use

Enter an area, a rate supported by your project analysis, and a documented adjustment factor. The calculator does not choose these inputs.

Calculation method

Load = area × chosen planning rate × adjustment factor

Variables

The adjustment factor can represent a documented scenario change. It does not replace envelope, solar, occupancy, ventilation, infiltration, humidity, or climate inputs.

Example

1,200 ft² × 20 BTU/h per ft² × 1.00 = 24,000 BTU/h.

Limits and disclaimer

This preliminary estimate is not Manual J or an ASHRAE load calculation and must not be used as final equipment sizing.

FAQ

Can I use a rule of thumb as final sizing?

No. Use a qualified professional and an appropriate detailed calculation for final design.
