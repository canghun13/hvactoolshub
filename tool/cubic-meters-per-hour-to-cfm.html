How to use

Enter a cubic meters per hour value.

Formula

CFM = m³/h ÷ 1.69901082

Variables

m³/h is metric airflow; CFM is cubic feet per minute.

Example

169.90 m³/h equals 100 CFM.

Result interpretation

This is a unit conversion only.

FAQ

Can I compare equipment after converting?

Yes, if rating conditions are otherwise equivalent.
