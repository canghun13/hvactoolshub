CFM Calculator

Calculate CFM from room volume and ACH.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Required airflowCFM

How to use

Enter inside dimensions and a project-specific ACH target.

Formula

CFM = length × width × height × ACH ÷ 60

Variables

Volume is in ft³; ACH is air changes per hour; CFM is cubic feet per minute.

Example

A 20 × 15 × 8 ft room at 6 ACH needs 240 CFM.

Result interpretation

This is a volume-flow estimate, not final equipment or code selection.

FAQ

Does this set a required ventilation rate?

No. Use applicable codes and a qualified design professional.
