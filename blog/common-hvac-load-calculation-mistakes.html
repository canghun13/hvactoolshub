Common HVAC Load Calculation Mistakes

Most errors begin when an input, scope, or design assumption is left undocumented.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Using area as the entire model

Area cannot replace climate, envelope, windows, solar, air leakage, people, equipment, humidity, and ventilation inputs.

Mixing units or conditions

Keep design temperatures, capacity units, and calculation assumptions consistent and traceable.

FAQ

Can a spreadsheet make a calculation detailed?

Only if its inputs and method are appropriate and verified.
