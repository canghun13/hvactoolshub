Load calculation guide

How to Calculate HVAC Load

Detailed loads combine building, climate, and operating inputs; floor area alone is only a preliminary planning input.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Identify design conditions

Use appropriate indoor setpoints and local outdoor design conditions before assessing loads.

Account for heat gains and losses

Envelope, windows, orientation, solar, infiltration, ventilation, moisture, occupants, lighting, and equipment all affect the result.

Use the appropriate procedure

Manual J is a residential load-calculation procedure; ASHRAE methods address detailed heating and cooling load calculation. A planning rate is not a substitute for either.

FAQ

Can I calculate an HVAC load from square footage?

You can create a preliminary estimate, but not a complete design load.
