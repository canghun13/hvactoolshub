Heating Load Calculation Explained

Heating load is the required rate of heat input at selected design conditions.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Heat loss inputs

Outdoor design temperature, envelope U-values, area, infiltration, ventilation, and indoor setpoint shape heating load.

Internal gains are conditional

People, lighting, and equipment can offset heat loss in some circumstances but cannot be assumed to provide dependable design heat.

FAQ

Can a cooling result set heating size?

No. Heating and cooling peaks can be driven by different conditions.
