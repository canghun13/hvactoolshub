How to calculate CFM

A room-volume method for preliminary airflow planning.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Formula

CFM = room volume × ACH ÷ 60

Steps

  1. Calculate room volume in ft³.
  2. Use a project-specific ACH target.
  3. Divide the hourly volume by 60.

Example

A 2,400 ft³ room at 6 ACH needs 240 CFM.

FAQ

Can this replace a ventilation design?

No. It is a simplified volumetric calculation.
