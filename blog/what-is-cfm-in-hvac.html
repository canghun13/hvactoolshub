What is CFM in HVAC?

CFM is cubic feet of air moved each minute.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

CFM measures volume flow

CFM describes airflow rate. It can refer to supply, return, exhaust, outdoor-air, or filtered recirculated flow; those uses are not interchangeable.

CFM and room volume

With room volume, CFM can be expressed as ACH.

FAQ

Is more CFM always better?

No. Required airflow depends on the purpose, equipment, comfort, noise, and code.
