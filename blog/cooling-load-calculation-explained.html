Cooling Load Calculation Explained

Cooling load is the rate of heat and moisture removal needed to maintain selected indoor conditions.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

External gains

Conduction and solar gain through the envelope and fenestration vary with weather, orientation, construction, shade, and time.

Internal and air-side gains

People, lighting, equipment, ventilation, infiltration, and moisture all need consideration.

Why simple rates are limited

Area rates cannot model hourly solar behavior or latent load. Use a documented detailed method for final sizing.

FAQ

Is cooling load only temperature?

No. Humidity and internal gains can also matter.
