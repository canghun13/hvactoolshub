HVAC Load per Square Foot Guide

Load-to-area ratios are useful for comparing a documented scenario, not for copying a design to another building.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

What the ratio means

BTU/h per ft² is total calculated load divided by conditioned floor area.

What it hides

Climate, construction, windows, orientation, ceiling height, occupancy, ventilation, and infiltration can be very different at the same area.

FAQ

Is there one correct ratio?

No. A valid ratio remains specific to its source calculation.
