Common HVAC Duct Sizing Mistakes

Preliminary calculations become unreliable when airflow, dimensions, or resistance assumptions are left unverified.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Using area without airflow

A duct size is meaningful only with a required CFM and a selected design criterion.

Ignoring fittings and components

Elbows, transitions, dampers, grilles, filters, and equipment affect the total pressure requirement.

Assuming one rule fits every project

Velocity and friction criteria vary by system, acoustics, occupancy, and applicable local requirements.

FAQ

Can a calculator replace final HVAC design?

No. It supports an initial check and should be verified by a qualified professional.
