How many CFM do I need per room?

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Start with volume

Room CFM is commonly estimated as volume × ACH ÷ 60. A floor-area rule cannot account for ceiling height.

Use the room purpose

There is no universal room CFM. Use the applicable ventilation purpose, code, and equipment design data.

FAQ

Can I use cooling airflow as outdoor air?

No. They are different airflow categories.
