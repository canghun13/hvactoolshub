Formula and use

Area = π × (D ÷ 12)² ÷ 4. Divide CFM by the area in ft² to calculate velocity in FPM.

FAQ

Is this a final sizing chart?

No. It is a geometry lookup; final design also considers pressure loss, fittings, and system criteria.
