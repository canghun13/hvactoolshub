Formula and units

kW = BTU/h ÷ 3,412.141633
RT = BTU/h ÷ 12,000

Values are thermal-capacity conversions. Confirm whether any equipment kW label describes capacity or electrical input.

Standard and scope

NIST lists the International Table Btu as 1.055056 kJ. This page uses that unit relationship and the conventional 12,000 BTU/h refrigeration ton for comparison.

FAQ

Are table values equipment selections?

No. They are unit conversions and nominal capacity comparisons, not a site-specific load calculation.
