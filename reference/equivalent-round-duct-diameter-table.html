Formula

Dₑ = 1.30 × (W × H)^0.625 ÷ (W + H)^0.25, with dimensions in inches. This is an equal-friction approximation.

FAQ

Can I use this instead of a complete duct calculation?

No. Confirm final pressure loss, velocity, fittings, duct construction, and system requirements.
