HVAC Tonnage by Square Foot Reference

A tonnage-to-area ratio describes an existing scenario; it does not prescribe HVAC size.

Created 2026-07-16 · Updated 2026-07-16

Formula

Tons per ft² = load (BTU/h) ÷ 12,000 ÷ area (ft²)

Interpretation

Two buildings with the same floor area may require different capacity because climate, envelope, glazing, solar, people, ventilation, humidity, and operating schedules differ.
