Why no universal number appears here

Applicable velocities vary with HVAC system type, acoustic requirements, duct construction, and local/project standards. A published value without those conditions can mislead.

FAQ

Where should final criteria come from?

Use the project specifications, selected design method, applicable codes, and qualified HVAC review.
