Formula and scope

m³/h = CFM × 1.69901082
CFM = m³/h ÷ 1.69901082

This is a pure volume-flow conversion. Preserve the air-stream label: supply CFM converts to supply m³/h, not to outdoor or exhaust airflow.

How to use the chart

  1. Confirm the starting value is a volume flow, not an air speed or capacity.
  2. Use the matching row or the formula for values not shown.
  3. Round only to the precision appropriate for the design or product data.
  4. Convert other inputs consistently when using area, velocity, pressure, or room-volume formulas.

FAQ

Is this a design table?

No. It is a unit-conversion table. It does not choose an airflow target, fan, or duct size.
